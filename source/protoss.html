THE ETERNAL SWARM

Our history spans millennia. Our purpose is absolute.

THE OVERMIND

Born from the ancient Xel'Naga experiments, the Overmind was created to achieve perfection through assimilation. It unified countless Zerg strains into a single hive consciousness, guiding the Swarm across the stars in search of genetic perfection.

"I am the Overmind. I am the eternal will of the Swarm."

THE QUEEN OF BLADES

Sarah Kerrigan, once a Terran Ghost, was transformed into the Queen of Blades—the most powerful being the Swarm has ever produced. Her strategic genius combined with her psionic abilities made her an unstoppable force that reshaped the galaxy.

"I am the Swarm. Armies will be shattered. Worlds will burn."

THE CREEP

The Creep is the biomass foundation of all Zerg structures. This living carpet spreads across worlds, transforming hostile environments into perfect Zerg habitats. It provides nutrients to structures, accelerates Zerg movement, and serves as the nervous system of every hive cluster.

"Where the Creep spreads, the Swarm follows."
